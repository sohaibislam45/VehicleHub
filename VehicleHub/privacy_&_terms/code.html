Privacy & Terms

Welcome to VehicleHub. Your privacy and trust are paramount to us. These documents outline how we protect your data and the rules governing your use of our platform. We aim for transparency in all our legal agreements.

01. Privacy Policy

VehicleHub is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect when you use our vehicle booking services and how that data is utilized to enhance your experience.

Summary of Key Points

  • We only collect data necessary for booking and security.
  • We never sell your personal data to third-party marketers.
  • You have full control over your profile and data export.

02. Data Collection

When you register or book a vehicle, we collect specific identifiers to facilitate the transaction and comply with local regulations.

person Account Data

Name, email, phone number, and driver's license verification details.

location_on Usage Data

Vehicle location (during active rental), IP address, and browser type.

All sensitive financial information is processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways. VehicleHub does not store your full credit card numbers on our servers.

03. Terms of Service

By using VehicleHub, you agree to comply with the following usage requirements:

  1. Eligibility: You must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid, unrestricted driver's license.
  2. Vehicle Care: Users are responsible for maintaining the interior cleanliness and reporting any mechanical issues immediately.
  3. Prohibited Uses: Vehicles may not be used for racing, towing, or commercial transport of hazardous materials.

04. Liability Limitations

VehicleHub acts as a marketplace platform. While we perform vehicle inspections, we are not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages resulting from the use of vehicles rented through the platform.

"To the maximum extent permitted by law, VehicleHub's total liability for any claim arising out of or relating to these terms shall not exceed the total amount paid by the user for the specific booking in question."

05. User Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • check_circle The right to access and export your personal data.
  • check_circle The right to request deletion of your account and associated history.
  • check_circle The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
